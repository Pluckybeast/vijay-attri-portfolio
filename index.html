About Vijay Attri

Vijay Attri is a seasoned legal professional with a Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Legislative Law (B.A LL.B Hon's) degree from GLA University, Mathura. Admitted to practice across All India, he brings a robust 3 years of experience in navigating complex legal landscapes.

Specializing in a diverse range of legal fields, Vijay Attri is committed to delivering exceptional legal services. His practice areas encompass Corporate Law, Civil Litigation, Intellectual Property, Real Estate, Family Law, and Criminal Defense, ensuring comprehensive support for his clients.

A member of the Delhi High Court, Vijay Attri is known for his analytical acumen, strategic thinking, and dedication to achieving favorable outcomes. He believes in a client-centric approach, prioritizing clear communication and personalized legal solutions.
